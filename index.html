What is Coolbet Open?

What is Coolbet Open? Coolbet is a gaming company which strives to be fair, personal and open. We have created Coolbet Open in order to give players the best possible poker experience building on these principles. The first Coolbet Open was played in May 2018 and the event is planned to take place twice per year. The next Coolbet Open will be played on 23-29 May 2022.
